Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you make custom sizes or designs not shown on the site?
Yes. Most of our moulds and machines are built to a customer's exact measurements or drawings. Bring your specification or describe what you need and we'll quote it.
How long does an order take to complete?
Simple items like flat trays or single moulds can be ready in a few days. Larger machines or bulk mould orders typically take one to two weeks, depending on size and quantity.
Do you deliver outside Nairobi?
Yes, we can arrange delivery countrywide through courier or transport partners. Delivery cost depends on the size of the item and the destination.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa and cash. For larger orders, we typically request a deposit to begin work, with the balance payable on completion or collection.
Is there a warranty on your machines?
Every machine is test-run before handover and we stand behind our welding and fabrication. Reach out to us directly to discuss specific warranty terms for your order.
Can I visit the workshop before ordering?
Absolutely. We're based at Kariobangi Light Industries, Off Outering Road, Nairobi. Walk-ins are welcome during working hours.
